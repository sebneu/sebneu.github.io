Home

I am a computer science researcher, living in Vienna, Austria, working at the St. Pölten University of Applied Sciences.

In my current research I focus on different aspects of data management:

  • Data ecosystems: data architectures, data governance, data quality management.
  • Knowledge Graphs: information extraction and modelling with semantic technologies.

I defended my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2019 at the Vienna University of Technology; my thesis is centered around methods to integrate and enrich open data using semantic web– and machine learning-based technologies, supervised by Professor Axel Polleres.
